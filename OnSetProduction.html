URL Do Camera Crews Use AI On Set?
Site Author Team BeverlyBoy
Abstract This article talks about how AI can really help on a film set, but there are some things that the crew needs to be wary of. It can help with automating tasks, checking inventory, data syncing, managing technical aspects of the process, and much more! But this article makes it clear that AI is still technology and anybody who uses it on a film set needs to use encryption to avoid intellectual property theft or data breaches. Also, of course, there is the issue of AI taking away jobs for a lot of people who would typically be working on a film set.


URL Smarter sets, bolder stories: Why AI is the future of video production
Site Author Steve Rosa
Abstract This article includes Steve Rosa’s opinion on using AI out in the field when shooting a film. He talks about the popular opinion that AI will destroy creativity and artists having a voice in their work, but based on what he has seen while working on sets, he sees AI from a different point of view. He believes that AI’s capabilities outweigh all of the cons and can be a great asset on a film set. He discusses how AI can not only help the crew be efficient and quick with getting the shots that they want, it can help with light setups, sorting footage, and color grading so that the people working on set do not have to worry about things like this. Overall, he believes that AI will help empower directors and people on set, not replace them.
